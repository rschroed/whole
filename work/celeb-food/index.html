PEOPLE CelebFood App

— Design Strategy • Interaction Design • User Research —

In what was to be the first in a series of premium, paid mobile apps for PEOPLE, Celeb food leveraged the magazine's unique relationship with celebrities to create a fresh and fun way to discover great recipes.

I worked with the product and technical teams at Time Inc. to define the product visions. The initial request was simply an app based on the recipes they create with celebrities. I collaborated with a small team at Fell Swoop to generate concepts, document user stories, design the interface and conduct user testing.
