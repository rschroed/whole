Epicurious iOS Apps

— Design Strategy • Interaction Design • User Research —

Epicurious had long been one of the top recipes apps in the app store, but it's design had fallen behind the times and was in need of a refresh.

I worked on a team that collaborated with product managers, engineers, and the brand team at Condé Nast to re-envision the user experience of both the iPad and iPhone apps. I contributed to the design strategy, documented user stories, created interactive prototypes, conducted user testing, and collaborated with the visual design team to deliver fresh UX that drove higher engagement.
