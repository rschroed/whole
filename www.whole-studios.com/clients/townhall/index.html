Town Hall Turns Ten!

Dear friends,
March 17, 2009 marked the ten year anniversary of the first public performance at Town Hall. Our founders imagined a venue for arts, ideas, advocacy and culture that would be open and democratic; bustling and energetic; dignified, but unstuffy—a kind of living room for a curious, creative city. A decade and a few thousand events later, we still aspire to uphold this promise. What's the best way to project the next ten years? Spend some time on our anniversary page for a sense of the energy and vitality that has brought Town Hall from a slip of an idea to rich, vivid life—all in ten short years.

Wier Harman
Executive Director
